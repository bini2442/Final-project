How to Use This Website

  • View Products: Go to the "Products" page to see images of our items and a table with their prices.
  • Hover Effects: Move your mouse over rows in the table to see highlights.
  • Place an Order: Use the "Order" page to fill out your details and select your preferred item.
  • Form Fields: Fill in all required form fields correctly. Fields include full name, major, gender, hobbies, and comments.
  • Navigation: Use the menu at the top to easily switch between pages.
  • More Products: Visit the external store link in the Products page (Amazon, etc.) for more items.
